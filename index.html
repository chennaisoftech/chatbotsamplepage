Food Friend Chatbot POC - CHENNAI SOFTECH

This functions as a proof of concept for a Restaurant Chatbot, showcasing a minimum viable product (MVP) capable of receiving and monitoring orders. The chatbot is crafted using Dialogflow, MySQL on Azure, and FastAPI in Python.
